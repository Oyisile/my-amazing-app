Did you know?

One of the most commonly mentioned motivations for playing crossword puzzles is its link to staving off Alzheimer's. Crossword puzzles can help you enhance your executivefunction and working memory by allowing you to focus your attention on a specific activity regularly. All of these abilities enhance one's capacity to successfully manage the problems of daily living while remaining as self-sufficient as possible.
